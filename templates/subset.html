Generate Signature Reports

From this page you can select a subset of drug-induced gene expression signatures to visualize. Start by typing, in the appropriate text boxes, drug terms, cell line terms, and time points to include.

Click on the "Example" button to populate the form with an example query.

Click on the "Submit" button to submit the form for processing. The information entered will be posted the to server and then an interactive visualization of the subset of signatures will be displayed.
